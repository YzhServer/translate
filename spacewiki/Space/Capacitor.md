A Capacitor is a crafting component.

# Obtaining

## Crafting

Capacitors can be crafted using the following items:

* 1x [Plastic](Plastic)
* 2x [Conduit](Conduit)
* 2x [Aluminium Alloy Ingot](Aluminium-Alloy-Ingot)
* 4x [Aluminium Alloy Nugget](Aluminium-Alloy-Nugget)
