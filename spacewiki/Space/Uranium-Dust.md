Uranium Dust is a resource that is used to power [Nuclear Reactors](Nuclear-Reactor) when used in tandem with coolant. It is also a crafting component.

### Crafting

Uranium Dust can be crafted using the following items:

* 4x Cobblestone
* 2x Magma Block
* 2x Blaze Powder
* 1x Emerald
