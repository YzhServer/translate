Spam is a food item which has a saturation of 8 hunger bars. A [Titanium Can](Titanium-Can) is returned once the food is consumed.

### Crafting

Spam can be crafted using the following items:

* 2x Cooked Porkchop
* 1x [Titanium Can](Titanium-Can)
