A Chair is a crafting component, but can also be placed and sat on by right-clicking it.

# Obtaining

## Crafting

A Chair can be crafted using the following items:

* 1x Black Wool
* 2x [Plastic](Plastic)
* 2x [Titanium Ingot](Titanium-Ingot)
