Titanium Dust is a crafting component, which can be smelted into two [Titanium Ingots](Titanium-Ingot).

# Obtaining

## Pulverization 

Titanium Dust can be created within a [Pulverizer](Pulverizer), when supplied the following items:

* 1x Raw Titanium
