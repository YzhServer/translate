A solar helmet can slowly charge energized items in a player's inventory.

# Obtaining

## Crafting

A solar helmet can be crafted using the following items:
* 1x Daylight Detector
* 1x Iron Helmet
* 4x [Aluminium Alloy Ingot](Aluminium-Alloy-Ingot)
* 2x [Surge Proof Wire](Surge-Proof-Wire)
